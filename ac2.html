Degree of Comparison Adjectives


Conversion from Superlative to Positive:

Superlative:He is the tallest man in the office.
Positive: No other man in the office is as tall as him
Explanation: ‘No other man’ is equal to ‘tallest’.


Conversion from Comparative to Superlative:

Positive:This student is more attentive than any other child in my class.
Comparative: This student is the most attentive child in my class.
Explanation: In the comparative degree, A single student’s quality is compared to the quality of an entire lot represented by ‘any other’. ‘More attentive’ changes to ‘Most attentive.’


Conversion from Positive to Superlative:

Positive:Few women in our office are as smart as Alicia.
Superlative:Alicia is one of the smartest women. in our office.
Explanation: The transformation from positive to superlative happens through the phrase ‘one of the’. Smart changes to smartest.


Conversion from Superlative to Comparative:

Comparative: ‘Baseball’ is not the most watched sports in America.
Superlative: ‘Baseball’ is not more watched than the other sports in America .
Explanation:The transformation from Superlative to Comparative happens through the phrase ‘not more watched than the other sports’. ‘Most watched’ changes to ‘More watched’.
